FAQS


Who is Gudo for?

Gudo is designed for, but not limited to, Speakers, Presenters, Students and Teachers, Viewers and Listeners. Gudo is made for anyone!


How does Gudo Work?

Gudo is designed to be activated by the user when the user wants their audio interpreted into images. Gudo uses an advanced library to recognize your audio and accurately generate an image relative to the content your listening to.
