What is Gudo?

Gudo is a Visual Assistant that is equiped with Autosense speech recognition that enables Gudo to render images based off the context of the dialogue its listening to.


Once Gudo picks up on a keyword, it returns an image based on the what it has just heard.


With Gudo you can easily make any listening experience that much better by having Gudo generate images for you or your audience!


How can I use Gudo?

Gudo can be used in a number of ways.


1. Presenting and Speaking


2. Image Searching


3. HOW EVER YOU WANT!
